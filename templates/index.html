About

We understand that recieving a medical diagnosis can be an overwhelming process. Oftentimes, we find ourselves coming to grip with our circumstances. I remember when my dad first found out his prognosis, he told me his mind went blank for the rest of the conversation. It's almost as if everything goes blank in that moment and your mind just races. This reality is far too common for us to stand around. Hence, we are creating an application to help visualize touch and complex conversations with doctors.
